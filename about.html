About Us

Community is at the heart of everything we do. Starbird Coffee is more than just a business; it's a gathering place where ideas are born. As a small business we cherish the support of our community and strive to give back in meaningful ways. From supporting local artists by displaying their work in our cafe or donating to local charities. And all of our coffees and teas are fair trade to boot!

Our inviting atmosphere is your starry home away from home. Between private areas for those who like to get away from the hustle and bustle to Friday night live music from our favorite local musicians there is a little something for everyone.
