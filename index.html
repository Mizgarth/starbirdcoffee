The Cafe at the end of the Universe

Welcome to the Starbird Cafe, the oasis nestled at the end of the universe. As you step through the doorway you'll find yourself immersed in a realm at the end of the cosmos. The menu at Starbird Coffee is as diverse as the galaxies themselves. Choose from an array of celestial beverages, each with its own unique twist by mixing and matching from our large variety of flavors add-ons. We're not just a destination, we are a journey to the edge of existence. A place where travelers from across the cosmos gather to enjoy their early morning caffeine fix. There is a little something here for everyone!

Beyond our stellar coffee, discover a menu that aims to delight. Dive into our intergalactic selection of fresh made sandwich each made with fresh in-house bread. Savor our cosmic confections that mirror the wonders of the universe, with each bite revealing hidden depths of flavor.
